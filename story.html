Story


The story begins with a valiant knight who sets forth on a harrowing quest, driven by the haunting memory of his beloved sister, cruelly slain by monstrous fiends. With a heart forged in sorrow and a blade sharpened by vengeance, he traverses through shadowy forests, perilous dungeons, and ancient, forgotten ruins. Each encounter with the fearsome creatures tests his resolve, yet strengthens his spirit. As he battles through the darkness, every victory is a step closer to the final reckoning with the monstrous overlord who shattered his world. Guided by love and justice, the knight's journey is a poignant dance of fury and redemption, where he will not rest until the monsters pay dearly for their sins.
